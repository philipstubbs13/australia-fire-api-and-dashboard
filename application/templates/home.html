Visualizing Australian Bushfires

About

This project explores how wildfires (known in Australia as "bushfires") have affected Australia over time. For this project, we gathered data captured by NASA satellites, as well as various historical wildfire and climate data. We visualized the damage and loss that Australia has sustained historically, including during the most recent (2019-2020) bushfire season, and show how climate temperature and precipitation changes are affecting the country.
