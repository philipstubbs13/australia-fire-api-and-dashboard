Visualizing Australia Fires

About

This project explores how wildfires (known in Australia as "bushfires") have affected Australia over time. Specifically, for this project, we gathered data captured by NASA satellites and various historical wildfire and climate data to help visualize what Australia not only has been through historically but also what Australia has gone through with the most recent (2019-2020) wildfire season. Use the links to the right to learn more about this project.

We visualized the damage and loss that Australia has sustained historically, including during the most recent (2019-2020) bushfire season and show how climate temperature and precipitation changes are affecting the country.
