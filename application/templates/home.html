Visualizing Australia Fires Over Time

About

This project explores how wildfires have affected the country of Australia over time. Specifically, for this project, we gathered data captured by NASA satellites and various historical wildfire and climate data to help visualize what Australia not only has been through historically but also what Australia has gone through with the most recent (2019-2020) wildfire season. Use the links to the right to learn more about this project.
